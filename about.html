About Us

The Physiobot is our project proposal to develop a knee rehabilitation robot to assist patients in regaining full range of motion and strength following injury or surgery. Embodying the core tenets of physiotherapy, the device is designed to provide automated assistance that complements traditional recovery methods. Our goal is to offer a solution that not only augments the patient's healing process but also delivers personalized, accessible, and consistent therapeutic exercises, all within the comfort of their home or preferred environment. Future versions of Physiobot could provide immediate relief and support to an injured person on-site.
